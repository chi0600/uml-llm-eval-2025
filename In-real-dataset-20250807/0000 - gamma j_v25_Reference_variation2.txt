Add Item To Cart

Goal:
Customer adds item(s) in the cart.

- Customer
- System

Customer clicks the button or link to "Add To Cart".

Basic Scenario:
1. Customer clicks the button or link to add to the cart with a specified quantity.
2. System adds the item(s) to the cart.
3. System prompts Customer to edit quantity or remove the item from the cart.

5. System stores a cookie with the cart details.
6. Customer returns to product listings.

A1. Customer terminates the web browser window after adding item(s) to the cart.
A2. Customer returns to the weborder interface.
A3. System recognizes the cookie and proceeds to display the product listings with the existing cart items.

Postconditions:
The Customer has added item(s) to the shopping cart.
