**Title:** Remove Product – Removal Process

**Raw:**

Requirements:  
Remove Product

Goal:  
Remove a product from the system.

Actors:  
Sales Person  
Weborder System

Preconditions:  
Sales Person must be able to access the weborder system via a web browser with HTTPS.

Triggers:  
Sales Person clicks the button or link to “Remove Product”

Basic Scenario:  
1. Sales Person clicks the button or link to initiate Remove Product process.  
2. System prompts the Sales Person to select a product by searching or viewing a list of products.  
3. System validates the product information.  
4. System removes the product page and product information from the system.  
5. System displays confirmation or updated product page.

Postconditions:  
A product is deleted from the system.

---

**Clean:**  
**Actors:** Sales Person, Weborder System  
**Preconditions:** Sales Person can access the Weborder System via HTTPS.  
**Triggers:** Sales Person clicks the **Remove Product** button or link.  

**Flow:**  
1. Sales Person → Weborder System: Click “Remove Product”  
2. Weborder System → Sales Person: Prompt to select a product (search or list)  
3. Sales Person → Weborder System: Select product  
4. Weborder System → Weborder System: Validate product information  
5. Weborder System → Weborder System: Remove product page and information  
6. Weborder System → Sales Person: Display confirmation or updated product page  

**Alternative Scenarios:**  
- None

**Postconditions:**  
Product is removed and confirmation or updated product page is displayed.
