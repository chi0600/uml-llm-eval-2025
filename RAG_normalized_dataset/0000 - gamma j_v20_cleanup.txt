**Title:** Update Product Attributes – Attribute Update Process

**Raw:**

Requirements:  
Update Product Attributes

Goal:  
Update Product attributes within the system.

Actors:  
Sales Person  
Weborder System

Preconditions:  
Sales Person must be able to access the weborder system via a web browser with HTTPS.

Triggers:  
Sales Person clicks the button or link to “Update Product Attributes”

Basic Scenario:  
1. Sales Person clicks the button or link to initiate Update Product Attributes process.  
2. System prompts the Sales Person to select a product by searching or viewing a list of products.  
3. System displays all of the product attributes and allows Sales Person to update the product name, product id, product description, product price, update the product image, product availability, and/or the number of items in the inventory.  
4. System validates the product information.  
5. System updates the product page and product information within the system.  
6. System displays the newly updated product page.

Postconditions:  
The product attributes have been changed.

---

**Clean:**  
**Actors:** Sales Person, Weborder System  
**Preconditions:** Sales Person can access the Weborder System via HTTPS.  
**Triggers:** Sales Person clicks the **Update Product Attributes** button or link.  

**Flow:**  
1. Sales Person → Weborder System: Click “Update Product Attributes”  
2. Weborder System → Sales Person: Prompt to select a product (search or list)  
3. Sales Person → Weborder System: Select product  
4. Weborder System → Sales Person: Display current product attributes (name, id, description, price, image, availability, inventory)  
5. Sales Person → Weborder System: Submit updated attributes  
6. Weborder System → Weborder System: Validate product information  
7. Weborder System → Weborder System: Update product page and information  
8. Weborder System → Sales Person: Display newly updated product page  

**Alternative Scenarios:**  
- None

**Postconditions:**  
Product page and attributes are updated and displayed.
