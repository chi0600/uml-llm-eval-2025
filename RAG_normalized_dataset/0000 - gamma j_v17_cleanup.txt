**Title:** Logout Sales Person – Logout Process

**Raw:**

Requirements:  
Logout Sales Person

Goal:  
Logout the Sales Person account on the system.

Actors:  
Sales Person  
Weborder System

Preconditions:  
Sales Person must be logged-in on the system.

Triggers:  
Sales Person clicks button or link to “Logout”

Basic Scenario:  
1. Sales Person clicks the button or link to initiate logout process.  
2. System terminates the session cookie.  
3. System displays home page.

Alternative Scenario:  
None

Postconditions:  
The Sales Person is logged out of the system.

---

**Clean:**  
**Actors:** Sales Person, Weborder System  
**Preconditions:** Sales Person is already logged in.  
**Triggers:** Sales Person clicks the **Logout** button or link.

**Flow:**  
1. Sales Person → Weborder System: Click “Logout”  
2. Weborder System → Weborder System: Terminate session cookie  
3. Weborder System → Sales Person: Display home page  

**Alternative Scenarios:**  
- None

**Postconditions:**  
Sales Person session is terminated and the home page is displayed.
